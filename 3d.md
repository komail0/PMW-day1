# PMW Day 1 - 3D Reconstruction Exploration

## 🚀 Project Overview
This repository documents my learning and experimentation with AI-based 3D reconstruction techniques as part of PreserveMy.World (PMW).

The goal is to understand how real-world environments can be digitized using computer vision and Structure-from-Motion (SfM) methods.

---

## 🧠 What I Learned Today
- Basics of 3D reconstruction pipelines
- How Structure-from-Motion (SfM) works
- Role of image overlap in building 3D scenes
- Introduction to photogrammetry concepts

---

## 🏗️ Goal
To explore how AI and computer vision can help preserve cultural heritage by reconstructing real-world sites into digital 3D models.
